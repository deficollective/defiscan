# Summary

Borrow and Lending protocol on Ethereum. On Ethereum Mainnet 3 markets exist mainnet, Lido and EtherFi. Markets Lido and EtherFi exist to allow efficient parameters for borrow and lending while containerize the risk associated with leveraged staked ETH.
